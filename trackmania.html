Self Driving Car - Trackmania

This project was developed during AI project course of my studies in a group of 3 studies consisting of myself, Salih Ekici and Kyano Trevisan.

Technologies used:

  • OpenCV
  • Tensorflow
  • Python

My contributions:

  • Wrote the code to gather training data and label the data appropriately with the correct keystroke at the time the image was taken
  • Worked on the OpenCV solution to find the most optimal filtering techniques and parameters
  • Experimated with different layers for our neural network to increase accuracy

My thoughts:

This project served as a nice way to wrap up our course as we got to put together many different aspects of our studies into a final product. During this project I had the chance to combine all of my previous course' knowledge into one to make a product which is greather than the sum of its' parts.
