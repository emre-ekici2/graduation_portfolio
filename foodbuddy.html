FoodBuddy

FoodBuddy was developed during the DevOps course of my studies. Our class was tasked to build an application using C# and WPF with no limitations as to what we were allowed to build. I decided to build a MyFitnessPal clone with a slight improvement in my opinion.

What does it do?

FoodBuddy allows the user to create food items with a unit of measurement associated with a calorie value.

Users can then log their daily weight along with logging which foods they have consumed throughout the day. FoodBuddy will over time calculate a calorie goal for the user so they can lose weight at a healthy rate depending on their moving weight average and their moving calorie consumption average.
