WaterHub - Project 4.0

WaterHub was developed during project 4.0 for Cipal Schaubroek. The goal of WaterHub was to provide a central hub to access water level data and to monitor it through the usage of dashboards.

Technologies used:

  • NodeRed
  • PostgreSQL
  • React
  • Grafana
  • Arduino
  • Python

My contributions:

  • Created flows in NodeRed to automate retrieval of data from outside sources such as waterinfo.be
  • Created flows to create and manage the pub/sub system for our custom sensor
  • Created an AI model to fill in missing warning levels for bodies of water which did not have them
  • Created a separate model to trigger warnings during rainfall in case of a possible flood in certain areas

My thoughts:

Project 4.0 was the first project where I was placed in an inter-disciplinary team. It was a new experience to work with other students who had received training and education completely different from mine.

My team in a way changed my mindset as I learned more about their skillsets. One person does not have to do everything or shoulder a majority of the workload when every person on the team has their own skillset and value to provide.

Overall I believe this project helped me to develop as a better communicator and mediator in team settings.
