EliComp - Internship Project

EliComp was my internship project between February and May 2024. The goal of EliComp was to develop an application from the ground up to promote physical fitness among the employees at Elision.

The project was developed as a group of 2 students, Seppe Meeus and myself. Both of us are students of Thomas More Hogeschool in Geel.

This project was an instance in which I felt left behind in comparison to my group. I found it difficult at times to match the expectations of my groupmate as he had much more experience than myself in terms of development, though at first this was worrisome for myself, over time I came to appreciate this as it allowed to learn from not only my internship mentors but from my groupmate as well.

Our goal was to create an application where users would be incentivized to perform physical activities individually and also as groups to also promote teamwork and comradery. We also wanted to make sure users would continue using the application for a long period of time and not just stopping after a while.

Technologies used:

  • Java
  • Spring
  • Svelte
  • PostgreSQL

My contributions

  • Created the account registration flow
  • Created the home page of the application
  • Created the profiles for users
  • Implemented the streak system
  • Implemented the experience/reward systems
  • Integrated the Strava API to the application
  • Created the teams system

My thoughts

This internship was overall a success for myself. I learned a lot more than I thought was possible in a short period of time thanks to both my mentors and my groupmate, thus I have developed my skills as well as my mindset and communication abilities. I don't think I could have chosen a better internship project or location for myself. The challenges I faced and the lessons I have learned will always stay with me.
